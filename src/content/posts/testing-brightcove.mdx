I picked up a Brightcove account and I'm playing around with embedding a video into my blog. I'm very impressed with their platform and after evaluating many others, this is the best I've seen.

It's very affordable for a business that wants to do more than just YouTube. While there is nothing wrong with YouTube, it has limitations — Brightcove lets you monetize and control access to your videos in ways YouTube doesn't.

The embedded video below was a test clip featuring my daughter Katelyn. The Brightcove player from 2011 is long gone, but the spirit lives on — it was a good excuse to test the platform.
