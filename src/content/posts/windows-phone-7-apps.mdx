I stumbled on a list of Windows Phone 7 apps and their popularity rankings, and thought I'd share. So far, all my favorites are in good working order — Facebook, Twitter, Foursquare — all solid.

My hopes are high that the app market won't be flooded with junk the way the Android and iPhone stores have been, but I can already see the fart apps and other noise starting to creep in. Inevitable.

As a former Android G1 user, I'm delighted with the user experience. I'm spending less and less time navigating menus and I get in and out of apps quickly. It really is a **phone to save me from my phone**. So far, I've converted three people into making the switch — all of them were iPhone users who had what I describe as iFatigue. They were ready for a change.

I see Windows Phone 7 as a slow-growing platform that's going to start catching buzz in the next few months. Microsoft's stock has been downgraded repeatedly because they aren't "sexy," but between Phone 7, Windows 7, and Kinect — I think it's a great time to pay attention.

What do you think? Have you tried it yet?
