It's a safe bet that if you're reading my blog, you've heard of the Amazon Kindle. I've still got and love my original Kindle — it's helped me read more and faster. The ability to search books and carry an entire library around is profound.

The reason most people don't have one is the price barrier. The devices were expensive, but newer models have come down quite a bit. Did you know there's a **free Kindle app** for just about every device — mobile phones, iPads, even your PC?

What's important about this: there are **thousands of free books** available to anyone. These are often classics in the public domain, digitized by passionate fans who want to spread knowledge. Many of them are excellent sources of inspiration and learning.

If you haven't explored the free Kindle catalog, start there. No device required — just the app.
